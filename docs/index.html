Do not accept trash.

A lightweight CLI tool to generate disposable email addresses instantly. Keep your real inbox clean and bypass spam filters in seconds.

Get Started View Source

Blazing Fast

No registration required. Generate a usable email address in milliseconds straight from your terminal.

Clipboard Ready

We value your time. The generated email is automatically copied to your clipboard.

Local Session

State is managed locally. Your inbox persists until you explicitly decide to clear the crumbs.

Installation

$ go install github.com/kikukafandi/donat@latest

Requires Go 1.20+

Workflow

The "Bake, Eat, Crumbs" philosophy.

Step 01

Bake Identity

Generate a new random email address. The address is saved to your local session file and automatically copied to your clipboard for immediate use.

$ donat bake
[*] Baking new identity... Done.
[+] Identity: m82s@sharklasers.com
[+] Copied to clipboard.
Step 02

Eat (Check Inbox)

Check for incoming messages. If you provide an ID, it will fetch the full content of that specific email.

$ donat eat
ID   SENDER          SUBJECT
--   ------          -------
1    info@fb.com     Welcome to Facebook...
$ donat eat 1
FROM : info@fb.com
SUBJECT: Welcome to Facebook...
--------------------------------
Hi there, please confirm your account...
Step 03

Crumbs (Logout)

Clear the local session file. This effectively "logs you out" and forgets the generated email address.

$ donat crumbs
[+] Session cleared.
